About SocialGuard

SocialGuard is a tool built to help users identify fake social media profiles and guard against identity spoofing. In an era where online deception is on the rise, our mission is to empower people with tools to make safer digital decisions.

Our Mission

We believe every person deserves to feel safe when interacting online. SocialGuard uses advanced heuristics and social signals to help users evaluate whether a profile is legitimate, suspicious or clearly fake. We prioritize transparency and simplicity, making it easy for anyone to use our detection tool-no technical background needed.


Why We Built It

With the explosion of social media, it's become easier than ever for malicious actors to create fake profiles that imitate real individuals or brands. This can lead to scams, misinformation, and emotional harm. SocialGuard was created with the goal of using technology for good to make digital spaces a little safer for everyone.


Who We Are

We are a team of passionate developers, designers who care about cybersecurity, privacy, and social responsibility.
SocialGuard was built with the intention of evolving it into a reliable tool for public good.
